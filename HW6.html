Florida State Math Standards:

MAFS.4.OA.1.1

Description: Interpret a multiplication equation as a comparison

Example: interpret 35 = 5 x 7 as a statement that 35 is 5 times as many as 7 and 7 times as many as 5. Represent verbal statements of multiplicative comparisons as multiplication equations.

Cognitive Complexity Level: Level 1: Recall

MAFS.4.OA.1.2

Description: Multiply or divide to solve word problems involving multiplicative comparison

using drawings and equations with a symbol for the unknown number to represent the problem, distinguishing multiplicative comparison from additive comparison.

Level 2: Basic Application of Skills & Concepts.
