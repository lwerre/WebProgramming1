Annie's Life in Lists by Kristin Mahoney

Annie is a fifth grader who thinks it is her fault when her family decides to move to a little town from Brooklyn, NY. When she discovers a secret in the attic of her new home, she begins to wonder what really brought her family to Clover Gap.

The Ambrose Deception by Emily Ecton

Three students are randomly chosen to participate in a scavenger hunt with a ten thousand dollar prize. They race around Chicago, trying to be the first to solve all of their clues. As the scavenger hunt progresses, they begin to wonder if there's more to the scavenger hunt than they initially thought.

Ghost Attack by David Lubar

Lex is visiting his grandparents and cousin when he comes down with a mysterious rash. He's allergic to ghosts, and tries to avoid them but no such luck. One ghost needs his help to solve a mystery!
