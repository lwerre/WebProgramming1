I Survived the Great Molasses Flood by Lauren Tarshis

Carmen's family moved from Italy to Boston, and are living their new lives when an unusual disaster happens. A tank filled with molasses explodes near their neighborhood, sending dangerous waves of the sticky substance through the streets. Carmen is caught in the flood and must fight for her life. How will she do it?

Ben Franklin's in my Bathroom! by Candace Fleming

Benjamin Franklin accidentally travels into the future, to the 21st Century, and is amazed by all of the modern inventions. Olive and Nolan have to figure out how to return him to his own time before anyone figures out what has happened.

The Bicycle Spy by Yona Zeldis McDonough

Marcel loves to ride his bicycle, and dreams about competing in the Tour De France, despite it being cancelled due to the German occupation of France. One day while making bicycle deliveries, he discovers a secret that will change his world. Will he be brave enough to join the resistance movement and save his friend and her family from danger?
